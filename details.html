Upon exiting the terminal, a Four Seasons team member will be waiting to greet you and coordinate every step of your journey to Nevis. Days in the sun

Beach & Pools

A half-mile of golden sand on Pinney’s Beach, and three resort pools beneath the palms.

Watersports

Complimentary non-motorized watersports — kayaks, seaboards, boogie boards, snorkeling equipment and paddleboards.

Golf

The 6,776-yard Robert Trent Jones II championship course — 18 holes from the sea to the slopes of Nevis Peak, with 10% off green fees.

Eco-Bio Golf

A beachside mini driving range — practice your swing into the water with balls that turn to fish food within 72 hours.

Tennis & Pickleball

Courts at the Caribbean’s #1-ranked tennis resort.

Spa & Fitness

A Caribbean garden sanctuary — with 10% off spa treatments of 50 minutes or longer.
