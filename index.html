Destination

Four Seasons Resort Nevis

Cradled between a dormant volcano and the Caribbean Sea, Nevis is one of the West Indies' best-kept secrets — unspoiled, intimate, and impossibly serene.

The Island

Thirty-six square miles of rainforest, black-sand coves, and a single mountain wrapped in cloud. There are no crowds here — only calm.

Why Nevis

Somewhere rare, that asks nothing of you but to be present.

Travel

Getting to the island

Fly

Connect through St. Kitts (SKB) or Antigua (ANU). For flights, Brett Lafleur — 402.677.6578.

The Crossing

A private boat carries you across the narrows — 45 minutes of calm water, then the island.

When

Arrive by Thursday, May 6. Depart no earlier than Monday, May 10.

Notes

Light linens, evening attire, reef-safe sun care. A valid passport is required.
